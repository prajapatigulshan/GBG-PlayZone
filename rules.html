Game Rules

General Rules

  • Har player ko apna real in-game ID provide karna hoga.
  • Match time par na aane par automatic disqualification ho jayega.
  • Hacking, teaming, glitch use ya unfair advantage strictly banned hai.
  • Organizers ka decision final hoga.

Free Fire Rules

  • Emulator allowed nahi hai (mobile only).
  • Character skill allowed hai.
  • Teaming pakdi gayi to team ko ban kar diya jayega.

BGMI Rules

  • TPP/ FPP mode organizer decide karega.
  • VPN strictly banned.
  • Squad coordination ke bina random teaming allowed nahi.

Minecraft / PC Games Rules

  • No hacking mods or external scripts.
  • Only approved texture packs allowed.
  • Server rules follow karna mandatory hai.

Penalty Rules

  • 1st Warning → Point Deduction
  • 2nd Warning → Match Loss
  • 3rd Warning → Tournament Ban
