Tournament Rules

  • Each player must register before participating.
  • Players must follow the assigned game schedule.
  • Cheating or using hacks will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • Respect all players and admins during the tournament.
  • Team changes are not allowed once registration is complete.
  • All decisions by GBG PlayZone admins are final.

Note: These rules apply to all games including Free Fire, Minecraft, BGMI, and PC Games.
